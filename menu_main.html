The Red Wagon Menu

Please note that we use fresh, local ingredients and small producers wherever possible. Much of our fare is produced in house- we smoke and cure our own meats, make our sauces and sides from scratch, and strive to please with every plate that we send out. While our daytime menu is fairly consistent and full of our Red Wagon classics, our evening menu has a tendency to change quite regularly to reflect what is currently in season and locally available.

Take out is available throughout the day 9AM-2:30PM (see our brunch menu below).

Weather permitting: Our Patio is now open! It is first come, first served. Unfortunately we cannot allow dogs in the dining area.
